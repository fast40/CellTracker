How to upload experiments

  • Gather all of your images in a single folder.
  • Make sure each filename starts with some experiment identifier and ends with "-before" or "-after" and then the filename.
    • Good examples: "experiment-1-before.png", "001-after.png"
    • Note that there should be exactly two images for each experiment identifier.
  • Upload your folder, and this app will take care of the rest.
